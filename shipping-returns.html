Shipping & Returns

Shipping

Orders are processed within 1-2 business days. Standard delivery typically arrives within 3-7 business days after dispatch.

Express delivery options can be added at checkout when available for your region.

Returns

Unworn items with original tags may be returned within 30 days of delivery. Final sale items are not eligible for return.

Start a return by contacting support with your order number and the item you would like to send back.

Exchanges

For a different size or color, place a new order and return the original item. This keeps inventory accurate and speeds up delivery.
